What it does

Honestly, kind of a lot.

Everything below runs on your machine. Pick what you like, ignore the rest.

🎛️

DJ Mode

Two decks, a crossfader, EQ and tempo on each side. Load any song on either deck and mix like it's a real setup.

🎧

Spatial Audio

Drag a ball on a pad to place the sound wherever you want in your headphones. Sounds way cooler than it has any right to.

🌊

8 Visualizers

Radial, Bars, Wave, Galaxy, Earth, City, Aurora, Ocean. All beat-reactive. Set any of them as your desktop wallpaper.

🎚️

The best EQ on any music player. Period.

Full 6-band EQ with presets (Bass, Treble, Vocal, Electronic, Rock, Jazz…) — or throw the sliders out entirely and draw your own curve with the magic-wand canvas. Bass Boost with an intensity slider (1–24 dB, go as hard as you want). Pitch shift ±12 semitones. Reverb with a dry/wet knob. Effect modes for Nightcore and Slowed + Reverb baked in. If you can hear it, you can shape it.

🌀

8D Audio — real, not a TikTok gimmick

Put headphones on and flip it. The sound orbits your head — side-to-side, back-to-front, a full rotation that actually works because it's processed through a real stereo panner, not a fake preset. Every song instantly feels like a live show. Combine with Reverb and it's unfair.

📥

YouTube Import

Paste a link. Get the audio. That's it.

🎬

Video Background

Use any YouTube link or local clip as the app background. Chill aesthetic unlocked.

🎙️

SFX Stuff

Record your own, trim, EQ, export. Useful if you do streams or content.

✨

Monthly Wrapped

Your month in music, Spotify-style — but only counts time when you actually listened, not when it was paused in the background. Shows up near month-end.

📡

Share over Wi-Fi

Play a track on another device on your local network. Nothing leaves your house.

🛡️

Yours, only yours.

Runs 100% on your PC. No accounts. No analytics. No servers. The full source is on GitHub if you're curious.

🎨

Make it look how you want

Themes, custom fonts, custom CSS, hotkeys, OBS overlay, theater mode. Make it your thing.
